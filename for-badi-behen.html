Dear Badi Behen,

You try so hard to act strict. I know that face — the one you make when you're trying to be disappointed in me. But I also know what's underneath it. Because no matter how wrong I am, no matter how many times I've made things harder than they needed to be, you're still there. Every single time.

I think about all the pointless arguments we've had — over the TV remote, over absolutely nothing — and how Mummy would end up scolding both of us, and we'd just look at each other like two idiots who got caught. Honestly? Those are some of my favourite memories.

You helped me land my first job. You backed me through things I'm not even sure I deserved to be backed through. I'm still figuring things out, still stumbling — but I'm trying. I don't know when I'll be able to make you feel truly proud, but I want you to know that everything I do, some part of it is for you.

You're not just my Badi Behen. You're my constant. The one thing that stays the same no matter what else changes.

Your annoying but lucky sibling 🤗
